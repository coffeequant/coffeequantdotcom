Risk Neutral Pricing (Without the Confusion)

---

🎯 The Idea

We don’t price options using “real world probabilities”.

We price using a fake world where:

  • Everyone is indifferent to risk
  • Assets grow at risk-free rate
---

🧠 Why?

Because if pricing depended on opinions:

  • No agreement
  • No market

So we switch to a world where:

No arbitrage decides price.

---

📊 Simple Example

Stock today = 100

Tomorrow:

  • Up → 110
  • Down → 90

Option payoff depends on this.

We assign probabilities NOT based on reality…

But based on no-arbitrage.

---

⚡ Key Insight

Risk-neutral ≠ real world

It is just a pricing tool.
