Gamma: Why Good Traders Buy High and Sell Low

Most people get this wrong.

They think trading is about buying low and selling high.

Gamma trading flips that logic completely.

---

⚡ The Core Idea

If you are long gamma, you:

  • Buy when market falls
  • Sell when market rises

Yes — you are trading against the move.

---

📊 Intuition (Think Like a Trader)

Imagine market is oscillating:

  • 100 → 98 → 101 → 99 → 102

A long gamma trader:

  • Buys at 98
  • Sells at 101
  • Buys at 99
  • Sells at 102

He harvests volatility.

---

💰 Where Does Profit Come From?

Not from direction. From movement.

Gamma is a machine that converts:

Volatility → P&L

---

⚠️ The Hidden Rule

If you are long gamma:

  • ❌ Don’t cross the spread
  • ✅ Post bids when market falls
  • ✅ Post offers when market rises

If you keep paying spread → you destroy your edge.

---

🐕 Short Gamma = Chasing the Market

Short gamma traders:

  • Buy when market rises
  • Sell when market falls

You are always late.

Like chasing your own tail.

---

🧠 One-Line Memory

Long gamma = calm trader

Short gamma = stressed trader
