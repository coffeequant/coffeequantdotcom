Basket Options: The Correlation Trap

---

🎯 The Problem

Black-Scholes assumes:

Everything is lognormal.

But for baskets…

This breaks.

---

📊 Why?

Individual stocks → lognormal ✔️

Basket of stocks → ❌ not lognormal

---

🧠 Intuition

Correlation changes everything.

  • High correlation → basket moves like one asset
  • Low correlation → diversification

But Black-Scholes ignores this dynamic behavior.

---

⚡ What Traders Actually Price

Not volatility alone…

Volatility + Correlation + Dispersion
