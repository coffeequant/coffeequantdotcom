I just hate fridays….and as a good short blog/story writer I must warn this is just plain simple logical rant….so there is a close button on your browser window (if u are using Internet Explorer) / Tab (if firefox). Feel free to use it, otherwise……….hmm……strange……u have nothin to do…

0:29AM …..same time on Sunday / (Monday to be precise) I would be waiting for this damn day and again on friday I will be thinking of this cursed weekend…and it’s a for (;;) loop. Dependency factor on anything is never good, and I guess weekends become a dependency coz every 4 weekends u wait for ur drug dose (well ur salary cheque also known as drug addiction as per XYZ!). After 12 months u wait for that sick new year and after those silly resolutions it’s time to wait for sad feburary and again it all comes down to this very day – the planning day…..when we start planning on this very Friday and by Sunday night all the plans are burnt and trashed and some say NORMAL LIFE RESUMES.

So since we believe in Randomness so much why not have a random number generator…to decide next week which day is gonna be a holiday or let’s say which 2 days will be treated as non-working and rest working. Or some higher authority like Ben Bernake roll a dice (well he already does in making economic decisions) and whichever number comes that day and next one is taken as the weekend. Of course then places like TGIF would be renamed to TGI(?). That might be way cooler…and in India we will have Bejan daruwala predicting the outcome of this dice, and people will sometimes gamble on the possibility of 2 weekends happening on same days consecutively. It will open up a wide array of financial instruments and wide array of possibilities by one simple dice roll for deciding the weekend. And maybe after 100 years or so…or let’s say gazillion years saturday and sunday will be remembered as the olden days. I don’t know where this is going…but sure hope as hell…u are a sane person if u were able to read this shit…all along…
